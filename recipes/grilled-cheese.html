Grilled Cheese

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • Butter
  • 2 slices of your favorite cheese.

Steps

  1. First butter one side of each piece of bread

  2. place a frying pan on medium heat

  3. Put one pice of bread butter side down in the pan. Then place two pieces of cheese on top of the bread. Now add the last piece of bread butter on top of the cheese with the butter side up

  4. Flip the sandwich over when the underside is a golden brown

  5. When the second side is golden brown remove from the pan.

  6. Let cool slightly and enjoy
