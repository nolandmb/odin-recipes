Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

Ingredients

  • Large jar(32oz) of your favorite maranara sauce

  • 1 pound spaghetti

  • 10 to 12 meatballs

Steps

  1. In a large pot boil water

  2. Add spaghetti to the boiling waer

  3. Put meatbaalls in a second pot and cover with sauce. place on a medium heat and simmer until meatballs are done

  4. Once spaghetti if cooked throughly remove from heat and strain

  5. Add some of the pasta to a bowl and cover with sauce. Add 2 to 3 meatballs. Sprinkle on parmesan cheese to taste
