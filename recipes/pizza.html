Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 5 oz shredded mozzarella
  • 3 oz pepperoni
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 550 degrees F

  2. dust work area with flour

  3. Roll out dough until it is about 12 inches wide

  4. transfer dough to a pizza pan

  5. Par bake the crust for about 2 minutes

  6. Top dough with sauce, cheese, pepperoni

  7. Bake for 8 to 12 minutes until the edges of the crust are golden brown
